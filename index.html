Hi, I'm Nicole

I’m a certified Personal Trainer based in West London. I began my professional career as a fashion model, then transitioned into personal training.

After facing debilitating health issues, I dedicated myself to further invest in my education to help people make their own lives healthier and more enjoyable by reconnecting with their bodies, reclaiming their strength & mobility, and rediscovering their potential.

I strongly believe results on the outside first have to happen on the inside therefore I focus a lot on wellbeing and becoming stronger and less on the number on the scale.

Personalised Your Goal Is My Mission

Approach

Every program is tailored to your body and needs.

Whether it's athletic performance, health, body-building, or weight-loss, and whether you're advanced or never stepped in a gym before, I'll develop a program tailored to your body, goals, dreams, and lifestyle.

My instruction is not constrained by a certain rote series of sets and reps. Rather, it's an ever evolving workout that varies as you get fitter, which results in quick and safe progress and keeps things interesting in the meantime.

My Story Fitness is Life

Health and fitness have been my passion since early age. Having grown up as a competing athlete, functional training was an important part of my upbringing.

Shortly after college, I become a fashion model which, between reading countless books on fitness and nutrition and personally trying a multitude of programs developed by world-famous professionals, was an 8 years-long hands-on study on the best ways of maintaining perfect shape.

Finally, after personally facing debilitating health issues that threatened my ability to not only train and work, but simply move and sit upright, I decided to put my skills and knowledge to use and dedicate myself to helping people make their own lives healthier and more enjoyable by reconnecting with their bodies, reclaiming their mobility, and rediscovering their potential.

I'm always up on scientific findings related to fitness, health practices, and rehabilitation.
