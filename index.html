Hi, I'm Nicole

I’m a certified Personal Trainer based in West London.
I began my professional career as a fashion model, then transitioned into personal training.
After facing debilitating health issues, I dedicated myself to further invest in my education to help people make their own lives healthier and more enjoyable by reconnecting with their bodies, reclaiming their strength & mobility, and rediscovering their potential.
I strongly believe results on the outside first have to happen on the inside therefore I focus a lot on wellbeing and becoming stronger and less on the number on the scale.

Programme Body

Transformation

Body transformation is 3 months fitness journey where we will be focusing on totally transforming your physique and mindset and teaching you how to program your training and nutrition and how to sustain long-term results.

Body transformation is perfect for people who are dedicated and committed to doing what it takes over the course of three months and will require 100% commitment on training and nutrition.

Programme Pre & Post-Natal

Stay active and energised during your pregnancy and address all of your fitness needs along the way. The program focuses mainly on strengthening muscles that become weak and releasing muscles that become tight due changes that happen to our body in pregnancy.

For new moms, it’s important to come back to fitness with a good strategy and care to make sure your body has time to heal and recover safely.

My Story Fitness is Life

Health and fitness have been my passion since early age. Having grown up as a competing athlete, functional training was an important part of my upbringing. Shortly after college, I became a fashion model which, between reading countless books on fitness and nutrition and personally trying a multitude of programs was an 8 years-long hands-on study on the best ways of maintaining perfect shape.

The PT qualifications opened the door for me to work as a PT but it didn’t teach me how to train people that have different lifestyle, how to release tight muscles, how to train if some of your muscles are overworked/underworked etc.

My own injuries pushed me further to educate myself but also to understand why it happened and if there is any way for other people to not get injured during their fitness journey.

I was even mentored for a few months by amazing world-famous physical therapist -John Green who saw my interest and curiosity( I ask many questions, always ;)).

I still spend many hours a week on studying and improving my knowledge related to fitness, health and rehabilitation, and I won’t stop cause that’s the best way to become better coach and to provide the best service to my clients making sure everyone can achieve their goals no matter their lifestyle or physical barriers. .
